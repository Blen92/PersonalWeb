MY HOBBIES

Movies

I love watching movies it allows me to take a momentary break from life, and immerse myselves into a different world on the big screen, whether it be a world full of talking toys, superheroes or even a zombie apocalypse.

Movies make us connect with fictional characters we’ve never met before, allow us to empathise with them while we watch them struggle, bounce back and then save the day! Movies are also a good way to change your current emotional state, if you feel down just put on a happy and cheerful animated movie to lighten your mood, or if you need a laugh just throw on an amusing comedy movie, films always manage to cheer us up and that’s another reason why we love them.

My all time favorite is "Friends" sitcom television series I laugh so hard every time a see it. And recently i watched "money heist" and i loved it.

Music

Simply, listening to music helps me feel good. If I am sad or depressed, I can turn on music and the release of dopamine in My brains is going to make me feel better. Music, in effect, is a drug that provides us with feelings of joy and pleasure.

Music can also express your emotions without having to show them. Some people when they are listening to music the music makes them dance. Music is powerful in many other ways that could take your feelings away.

I listen to both Amharic and English songs. I am a big fun of Ethiopian 90's music.
